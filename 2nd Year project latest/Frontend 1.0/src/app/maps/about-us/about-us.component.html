About Us

INVENTO is the fixed assets management system of CodeGen international (Pvt)Ltd. Identify the assets' current location , report breakdowns, view assets' details, view employees's details, register new employees or assets generate reports, booking assets are the functions of Invento.

Our aim is to provide employees a best work environment when working with the assets of the company

If you want to know more about Invento, please use our contact us (current) page.

Authorities

Assets managet is the main authorized person of the system.

Only Asstes manager, CEO, Event organizers, Department heads can booked the assets.

Any registered employee of the company can request any asset through the system.

Only high management have the authority to generate reports.

Rules & Regulations

Employees should return the assets maximum in 2 weeks

Employees should take responsibility for the assets when it is under his/her responsibility

Any employee can report breakdowns to the authority
